Akira Toriyama Tribute Page

Timeline

Apr 5, 1955
Born in Kiyosu, Aichi Prefecture, Japan
Apr 1, 1974
Graduated from the design department of the "Prefectural Industrial High School"
Apr 1, 1977
Started working at "Shueisha", a manga publisher, as an artist
Dec 18, 1978
Debuted as a manga artist with the publication of Wonder Island in the #52 issue of Shonen Jump
Jan 18, 1979
Wonder Island published in Shonen Jump #25
Jan 18, 1980
Dr. Slump started with issue #5/6 of Shonen Jump
Mar 18, 1981
Pola & Roid, published in Shonen Jump #17
Jan 18, 1982
Escape, published in Shonen Jump Special #10
Feb 18, 1983
Chobit, published in Fresh Jump
Nov 18, 1984
Dragon Ball manga burst into Shonen Jump, in issue #51
Dec 18, 1985
Dragon Ball vol. 1 published
May 18, 1986
TV game Dragon Quest goes on sale (Toriyama in charge of character design)
Jul 18, 1987
Movie premiere of Dragon Ball - The Sleeping Princess of Demon Castle
Apr 18, 1988
Airing of Apple Pop puppet theater on "Hirake! Ponkikki" children's TV (Toriyama in charge of puppet design)
Apr 18, 1989
Dragon Ball TV series ended
Apr 26, 1989
Dragon Ball Z TV series started
Jan 31, 1996
Dragon Ball Z series ended
Mar 1, 2024
Dies at the age of 68
